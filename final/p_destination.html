Myeongdong

Myeongdong, Seoul's vibrant shopping district, offers endless shops, street food, and entertainment, making it a must-visit for South Korea's shopping culture.

3-4 hours Suggested Time

Hongdae

Hongdae, Seoul's vibrant youth district, offers arts, music, cafes, street performances, and trendy shops, making it a must-visit for students and young artists.

1 day Suggested Time

N Seoul Tower

A cable car takes you to the tower's base, offering four observation decks, five restaurants, and an immersive media art exhibition showcasing Seoul.

1 day Suggested Time

Gangnam Seoul

Gangnam, Seoul's glamorous district, is known for its upscale shopping, vibrant nightlife, and fine dining, embodying Seoul's modern, luxurious side.

1 day Suggested Time

Gyeongbokgung Palace

Gyeongbokgung Palace showcases the grandeur of the Joseon Dynasty with ornate sculptures, serene gardens, and tranquil ponds.

1 day Suggested Time

Insadong

Insadong, Seoul's cultural haven, blends tradition and modernity. Explore wooden tea houses, savor award-winning cuisine, and admire traditional art in boutique galleries.

1 day Suggested Time
